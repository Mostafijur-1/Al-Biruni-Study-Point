## 📖 About Al-Biruni Study Point (ABSP)

**Al-Biruni Study Point (ABSP)** is a modern, responsive coaching center website and interactive Learning Management System (LMS) designed for secondary (SSC - Grades 9–10) and higher secondary (HSC - Grades 11–12) science students in Bangladesh. 

The platform supports a native, bilingual experience (Bangla and English) and offers structured academic guidance, digital class materials, automated examinations, and grading portals to bridge the gap between classroom teaching and digital learning.

---

## 🏛️ Website Portals & Capabilities

The platform is divided into distinct portals catering to different audiences:

### 1. Public Marketing Website
Designed for parents, prospective students, and visitors to browse core offerings:
- **Batches & Schedules**: Real-time listing of active online and offline batches, schedules, and class limits.
- **Course Catalog**: Detailed breakdown of SSC and HSC subjects, curriculum highlights, and course fees.
- **Tutor Profiles**: Professional bios, qualifications, and domain experience of lead educators.
- **Support & FAQ**: Answers to common questions, direct contact forms, and coaching address details.

### 2. Student LMS Dashboard
A personalized workspace where registered students manage their science preparation:
- **Recorded Class Library**: Access to structured, video lectures to learn and revise topics at their own pace.
- **Practice MCQ Exams**: Timed interactive quizzes to practice multiple-choice questions with instant scoring, solutions, and explanations.
- **Written Assignments (CQ)**: A portal to submit photos of handwritten creative papers for teacher evaluation.
- **Performance Reports**: Direct access to exam histories, grades, and personalized feedback.

### 3. Teacher Portal
An educational workspace for teachers to manage courses and evaluate performance:
- **Written Question Review**: A grading viewport where teachers can review student-submitted images, assign marks, and write constructive feedback.
- **Exam Analytics**: Overviews of class-wide and student-specific test results to identify learning gaps.
- **Class Resource Management**: Tools to upload recorded lectures, class materials, and study notes.

### 4. Administrative Control Center
A management dashboard for platform administrators:
- **User Registrations**: Manage student files and control teacher profile verifications and approvals.
- **Course & Batch Configuration**: Set class timetables, capacities, and update fees.
- **System Analytics**: Platform-wide metrics on enrollment rates, active learners, and overall performance.
